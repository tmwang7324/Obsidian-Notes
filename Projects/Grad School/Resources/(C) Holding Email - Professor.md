## Notes
- **Why ask about Interfolio:** a dossier service lets her write one strong letter and reuse it across programs and cycles — the real version of "reusable." (Letters are *not* handed to me as a PDF to self-submit; recommenders submit directly through each program's portal, so a stored/dossier letter is the closest thing to reuse.)
- **Why recency matters:** letters go stale — her specific memory of me fades, and committees weight recency. Getting the list to her this fall keeps the letter specific and enthusiastic.
