I have began to notice that the Ideas and Resources directory within each project directory shares a lot of functionality with the notes and assets directory in raw/. 

In both raw/ and Projects/, I am supposed to record things that I find meaningful while doing specific projects. Both folders even have a folder that stores resources (assets/ in raw/ and Resources/ in Projects/). I am unsure whether or not to keep the two directories separate.

In raw/notes, I imagine myself recording explanations of complex topics, key implementations, and significant tips.

In Ideas, I believe I should be only recording my reasoning, design decisions, and debate points against AI.


## Keeping raw/ and Projects/ separate
### Advantages
---
* Clear separation for synthesis and /grill-me
* Keeps the primordial raw folder

### Disadvantages
---
* Why not just have a notes folder for projects?
* Thin line of separation
* AI must traverse through root directories
