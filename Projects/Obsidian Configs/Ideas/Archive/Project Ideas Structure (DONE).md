
### Machine + Human 

Instead of separating hand-typed notes such as this file into a different folder, keep AI summarized grill-me sessions, significant chat exchanges, and human typed notes in Ideas together. Ideas should contain files prefixed by "(C)" and normal markdown.

* AI does not need to hop around folders to synthesize data. 

#### Disadvantages:
* AI reads its own output (redundant)
* The Chat folder already holds AI conversations
