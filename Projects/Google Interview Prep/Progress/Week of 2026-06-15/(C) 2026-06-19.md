# (C) 2026-06-19 — Trees-wrap traversal write-ups, roadmap reordered to front-load graphs + DP

**Advances:** [[(C) Google SWE Offer Roadmap|Google SWE Offer Roadmap]]

## Done
- Wrote up the four core **tree-traversal** patterns — **preorder** (Root→L→R, tree copies/prefix exprs), **inorder** (L→Root→R, BST ascending order), **postorder** (L→R→Root, bottom-up tasks/RPN), and **BFS level-order** (queue, layer-by-layer) — each with O(n) complexity, use-cases, and recursive implementations.
- Added **iterative** versions: iterative preorder (explicit stack), and worked through the hard iterative **inorder** and **postorder** "dive" algorithms — using `last_visited` to detect when a parent's children are all processed — keeping the broken attempts documented in-note for reference.
