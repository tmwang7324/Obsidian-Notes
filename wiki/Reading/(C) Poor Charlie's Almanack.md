# Poor Charlie's Almanack

A collection of the wit and wisdom of **Charlie Munger** — Warren Buffett's right-hand man and vice-chairman of Berkshire Hathaway.

## Charlie Munger
- An exceptional **polymath** with encyclopedic knowledge spanning ancient civilizations to pop culture.
- Known for a latticework of **interconnected yet simple-to-understand mental models** that guide his decisions, and for his mastery in presenting those models and his ideas.
- Widely described as the closest man to a "**modern-day Benjamin Franklin**" — the figure Munger himself idolizes.
