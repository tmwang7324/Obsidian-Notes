# (C) Helena Follow-Through Tracker

The repair conversation (2026-06-21) was a *bid*, not a verdict. This is where the **test phase** gets logged — behavior over weeks, not feelings in the moment. Two reasons it exists:

1. **Judge by weeks, not minutes.** Your discernment rule, applied with evidence instead of memory. Does she actually adjust on D and E, or quietly resume the old standards?
2. **You lose recollection of what sparked major arguments.** So every incident gets logged *with its circumstances* the same day — trigger, what was actually said/done, who held or folded. Future-you can reconstruct the fight instead of arguing about whose memory is right.

> **How to log:** add an entry under **Incident log** the *day it happens* (memory decays fast — that's the whole point). Tag the boundary it touches: **A** (over-referencing), **D** (lash-out), **E** (time/autonomy), or **watch** (popcorn/nosy-type pattern data). Keep it factual — what happened, not how you felt about it.
